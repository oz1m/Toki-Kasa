# Phonotactics
---
## Syllable Structure

All words will be composed by less than 5 syllables, where each syllable is a consonant followed by one or two vowel, where vowels have to be different from each other (*CV* / *CVV*): 
- **guare** = gua-re
- **kenici** = ke-nu-ci
- **rikae** = ri-kae

> *CVV* syllables are diphthongs

> [!warning] No codas are allowed.

---
## Word structure
Each word start with any consonant and ends with any vowel or any diphthong.
